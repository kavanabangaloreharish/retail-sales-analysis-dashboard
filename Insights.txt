Retail Sales Analysis — Key Insights

Top Revenue Products
The Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier is the clear winner here, bringing 
in $61.6K — way more than double whatever's in second place. Most of the 
top 10 products are office equipment like copiers, printers, and binding 
machines, not everyday office supplies. Makes sense, since these are 
big-ticket items.

Region Performance
West is the strongest region overall, leading in both sales ($725K) and 
profit ($108K), with East close behind. What's interesting is South — it 
has the lowest sales of all four regions but still beats Central on profit. 
So Central is moving more product than South but keeping less of it as 
profit. Worth digging into why (probably discounting, but I'd want to check 
in SQL before assuming).

Customer Segments
Consumer is the most profitable segment by a good margin — about $134K, 
compared to $92K for Corporate and just $60K for Home Office. So individual 
buyers are actually driving more profit than business clients, which I 
didn't expect going in.

Sales Trend Over Time
Sales basically stayed flat from 2014 to 2015, then took off — growing from 
$484K in 2014 to $733K by 2017. So the real growth story is in the last two 
years, not a steady climb the whole time.
